About Us. Just two guys with a dream.

Omaha 3D Print was founded by Nathan and Tobias Davis with the goal of becoming Omaha's local 3D printing resource. Since then Nathan Davis has moved to KüL 3D to have a greater impact on the 3D printing community; thus, Omaha 3D Print officially no longer offers services. If you need 3D printing, scanning, or engineering services, contact KüL 3D.

With the power of 3D printing a digital model can be turned into a physical object you can hold in your hands. 3D prints are created out of thin air (thick plastic, actually) from a 3D model file. You can find many models already made at Thingiverse. If you don't have the file, KüL 3D offers consulting services to help design your creation.
